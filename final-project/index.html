Prime Music
Subsribers have access to thousands of songs via smartphone or tablet.

Prime Photos
With subscription, members get access to store photos and videos.

Prime Now
At this point, members can get free two-hour delivery and one-hour delivery for a low price.

Prime Reading
Amazon introduces a virtual platform for reading and browsing through thousands of different books.

Amazon Visa Card
Prime members can apply for a visa card to use as a form of payment.

Prime Wardrobe
Prime members can purchase clothes through Amazon, try them on and return them without any additional charges.

Amazon Key
